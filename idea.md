HealthOS is an AI Medical Memory platform that transforms fragmented medical records into a lifelong, intelligent health memory owned by the patient. Today, every consultation starts from scratch—patients repeatedly explain their medical history, doctors spend valuable time searching through reports, and critical context is often lost between hospitals, specialists, and insurance providers. HealthOS changes this by continuously building a structured understanding of a patient's health from medical records, prescriptions, lab reports, wearable devices, lifestyle data, and doctor conversations. Instead of storing documents, it stores relationships and longitudinal context.

Using Gemma as the multimodal reasoning engine, HealthOS extracts and understands information from reports, prescriptions, and conversations, creating a persistent Health Knowledge Graph that evolves with every interaction. 

At the hackathon, we will build an end-to-end prototype where a patient uploads years of medical history and wearable data, and within seconds HealthOS generates a complete AI medical memory. A doctor receives an intelligent consultation dashboard with relevant history, medication interactions, longitudinal health trends, and suggested follow-up questions before the consultation even begins. The patient receives personalized explanations, medication guidance, and preventive recommendations in natural language. Our vision is to move healthcare beyond Electronic Health Records toward Electronic Health Memory—where AI doesn't just answer questions about your health, it remembers your health.
